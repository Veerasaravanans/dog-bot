┃                           ⚠️ CRITICAL WARNINGS ⚠️                          ┃
┣━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┫
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  1. REMOVE ENABLE JUMPERS BEFORE CONNECTING GPIO 2 & 4                     ┃
┃     ► The L298N ships with jumpers on ENABLE A and ENABLE B                ┃
┃     ► These jumpers tie ENABLE pins to 5V (full speed, no PWM)             ┃
┃     ► You MUST physically remove both jumpers for PWM control              ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  2. CONNECT ALL GROUNDS TOGETHER                                           ┃
┃     ► ESP32 GND, L298N GND, Battery - must be common                       ┃
┃     ► This prevents voltage spikes and ensures proper operation            ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  3. VERIFY MOTOR POLARITY                                                  ┃
┃     ► Test each motor direction before final assembly                      ┃
┃     ► Swap motor wires at L298N if direction is reversed                   ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  4. CHECK BATTERY VOLTAGE                                                  ┃
┃     ► 4x AA batteries = 6V nominal (5.2V-6.4V range)                       ┃
┃     ► L298N accepts 5V-35V on +12V input                                   ┃
┃     ► Below 5V: motors may not run; Above 7V: use L298N's 5V regulator     ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┗━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┛

┃                           PWM SPECIFICATIONS                                ┃
┣━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━┫
┃  Frequency:        1000 Hz (1 kHz) - inaudible, smooth operation           ┃
┃  Resolution:       8-bit (0-255)                                            ┃
┃  LEDC Channel A:   0 (GPIO 2 → ENABLE A)                                   ┃
┃  LEDC Channel B:   1 (GPIO 4 → ENABLE B)                                   ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  Speed Mapping:                                                             ┃
┃    0   = Full stop (0% duty cycle)                                          ┃
┃    64  = 25% speed (25% duty cycle)                                         ┃
┃    128 = 50% speed (50% duty cycle)                                         ┃
┃    192 = 75% speed (75% duty cycle)                                         ┃
┃    255 = Full speed (100% duty cycle)                                       ┃

┃                                                                             ┃
┃  ❌ Motors run at full speed regardless of speed parameter                 ┃
┃     ► ENABLE jumpers still installed → Remove them!                        ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  ❌ Motors don't move at all                                               ┃
┃     ► Check ENABLE A/B connections (GPIO 2 & 4)                            ┃
┃     ► Verify battery power (should be ~6V)                                 ┃
┃     ► Check IN1-IN4 direction pin connections                              ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  ❌ Motors spin in wrong direction                                         ┃
┃     ► Swap the two motor wires at L298N screw terminals                    ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  ❌ L298N gets very hot (>80°C)                                            ┃
┃     ► Normal to be warm (50-70°C), but not burning hot                     ┃
┃     ► Check for motor stall or mechanical binding                          ┃
┃     ► Reduce speed or add cooling fan if needed                            ┃
┃                                                                             ┃
┃  ❌ Serial Monitor shows errors                                            ┃
┃     ► Check WiFi credentials (lines 14-15 in firmware)                     ┃
┃     ► Verify MQTT broker settings (lines 18-21 in firmware)                ┃
